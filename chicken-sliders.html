Chicken Sliders

Burgers Party Food Prep: 40 min

Sauce

Ingredients

  • Coriander
  • 2 limes
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 4 chilies
  • 2 shallots
  • Water
  • Rice vinegar
  • Salt

Cucumber Salad

Ingredients

  • Cucumbers
  • Sugar
  • Sesame seeds
  • Sesame oil
  • Rice vinegar
  • Soy sauce
  • Fish sauce
  • Chili flakes

Instructions for sauce

  1. Add the coriander, garlic, chilies, and shallots into a blender.
  2. Squeeze in the juice of the limes.
  3. Add some water, rice vinegar, and salt.
  4. Blend everything together.
  5. Strain the mixture through a sieve into a saucepan.
  6. Place the saucepan on heat, add honey, then set aside.

Instructions for Cucumber Salad

  1. Cut the cucumbers.
  2. Add sugar, sesame seeds, sesame oil, rice vinegar, soy sauce, fish sauce, and chili flakes.
  3. Mix everything together.

Instructions for chicken

  1. Coat the chicken in Oak’a Sweet Garlic Spice Mix.
  2. Cook the chicken in an air fryer.
  3. Add the cooked chicken into the sauce.
  4. Glaze it from both sides.
