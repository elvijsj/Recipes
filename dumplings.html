Dumplings

Comfort Food Latvian Prep: 30 min

Ingredients

  • 500 g dumplings
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Sour cream for serving

Instructions

  1. Bring salted water to a boil.
  2. Add the dumplings and stir gently.
  3. Cook until they float and are fully cooked through.
  4. Drain and serve hot with sour cream.
