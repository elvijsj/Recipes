Iraklijs

Spicy Chicken Prep: 45 min

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Potatoes
  • Champignons (mushrooms)
  • Red onion
  • Red sweet pepper
  • Leek

Spices

  • Oak'a Delicious Chicken
  • Oak'a Sweet Garlic
  • Oak'a SPG
  • Oak'a Spicy BBQ

Sauces

  • Oak'a Cherry glaze
  • Oak'a Asian chilli sauce

Instructions

  1. Cut the chicken thighs into strips and start cooking.
  2. Finely slice the potatoes and cook them in an air fryer.
  3. Slice the mushrooms and add them to the chicken.
  4. Add red onion cut into half-rings to the chicken.
  5. Add red sweet pepper cut into long strips to the chicken.
  6. Finely chop the leek, but do not add it yet.
  7. Season with the spices: Delicious Chicken, Sweet Garlic, SPG, and Spicy BBQ.
  8. Add the sauces: cherry glaze and Asian chili sauce, and cook until everything is glazed.
  9. At the end, add the leek once everything is glazed.
