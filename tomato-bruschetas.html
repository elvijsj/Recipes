Tomato Mozzarella Bruschettas

TAG 1 TAG 2 Prep: XX min

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Basil
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Balsamic
  • Red pepper
  • Oregano
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Honey
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Bread

Instructions

  1. Blend the tomatoes, but not too much — leave some larger pieces in there.
  2. Strain off the excess liquid so only the thick part remains.
  3. Finely chop the basil and garlic.
  4. Mix everything together, add the spices, and a little bit of honey.
  5. Lightly toast the bread.
  6. Place mozzarella on the bread, add the tomato mixture, and garnish with a bit more basil.
